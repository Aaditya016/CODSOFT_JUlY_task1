Autumn Season

Autumn is a beautiful season that marks the transition from summer to winter. The days start getting shorter and the nights longer, and the leaves on the trees start to change color. This is a time for harvest, as farmers bring in their crops of corn, soybeans, and pumpkins. Autumn is also a time for festivals, such as Halloween and Thanksgiving.

Winter Season

Winter is the coldest season of the year, and it is characterized by shorter days and longer nights. The days are often cloudy and overcast, and the weather can be quite unpredictable. However, winter can also be a beautiful season, with snow-capped mountains, frosted trees, and sparkling ice.

Summer Season

Summer is the warmest season of the year. It is a time for long, hot days and short, cool nights. The sun is up for longer hours, which means there is more time to enjoy outdoor activities. Many people go on vacation during the summer, and many children have summer vacation from school.

Spring Season

Spring is a season of renewal and new beginnings. It is the time of year when the days start getting longer, the weather starts to warm up, and the flowers start to bloom. This is a time for people to get outside and enjoy the fresh air and sunshine.
